Overview:-
Aestex-The smart apparel shopping system


Our aim is to deliver queueless, counterless shopping system to help fast and smart shopping.It will convey enhanced shopping experience for both the customers and shop owners.
No more need to wait in the long queues for dressing room trials. Our system will be a quick and rousing practice for experimental shoppers. It can ensure the perfect buy everytime with Digital online dressing attribute (on 3d figure) .
Incorporating nearly human-less methodology (leading to less investment or initial capital).Also keeping up with cashless India programme.

Features:-

- Barcode generation (By shop owners).
- Effortless barcode scanning 
- Quick/automated payment.
- Shows similar product recommendation.
- Virtual dressing room (with AR).
- Secure payment gateway.
- (Contactless shopping methodology.)
- AI/ML based approach.
